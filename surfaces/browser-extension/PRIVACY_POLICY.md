# Privacy Policy — Thirsty (AI Water Meter)

_Last updated: 2026-07-01_

Thirsty is designed to collect **nothing**. It runs entirely inside your browser.

## What data the extension accesses

- On `claude.ai` pages, Thirsty reads the **visible conversation text** (your
  messages and Claude's replies) to estimate token counts. This reading happens
  locally, in the page, and only to compute the on-screen meter.

## What the extension does with it

- It converts that text into an estimate of water / energy / CO₂ and renders a
  small card. That's it.

## What the extension does NOT do

- **No servers.** Thirsty makes no network requests. Your conversation text never
  leaves your device.
- **No storage of your content.** It does not save, log, or transmit your prompts
  or Claude's replies.
- **No tracking, analytics, ads, cookies, or fingerprinting.**
- **No selling or sharing of data** — there is no data to sell or share.

## Permissions

Thirsty requests access only to `https://claude.ai/*`, solely to display the meter
on that site. It requests no other permissions (no tabs, history, clipboard, or
background access).
